# Kadal2Kadaai (K2K)

**Kadal2Kadaai** (From Sea to Shop) is a premium South Indian marine commerce platform that bridges the gap between local fishermen and consumers. Our mission is to deliver traceable, premium-grade marine catch directly from the harbor to your kitchen, eliminating cold-storage middlemen and ensuring maximum freshness.

## What This Website Does

This platform serves as a dual-sided marketplace and operational tool. It provides consumers with an intuitive, modern interface to purchase fresh seafood online, while simultaneously providing administrators with a robust backend portal to manage inventory, products, orders, and users.

## Key Features & Functions

### 1. Consumer Marketplace
- **Dynamic Product Catalog:** View fresh catch with high-quality images, pricing, and availability.
- **Advanced Filtering & Search:** Easily search for specific seafood or filter by categories and sort by price, popularity, or freshness.
- **Product Details:** Detailed pages for each catch, including origin, freshness guarantee, and seller information.
- **Secure Shopping Cart & Checkout:** A seamless cart drawer and checkout flow supporting order notes, multiple delivery addresses, and Cash on Delivery.
- **Modern UI/UX:** A "Deep Ocean" aesthetic utilizing glassmorphism, smooth animations, and responsive design for a premium shopping experience.
- **Trust Indicators:** Clear transparency on sourcing ("Direct from Harbor", "Same Day Delivery", "Quality Verified").

### 2. Admin Operations Portal
- **Dashboard Overview:** Monitor platform health, recent orders, and key metrics.
- **Inventory & Product Management:** Add new catch, update stock levels, manage pricing, and categorize seafood.
- **Order Processing:** Track, fulfill, and manage customer orders from placement to delivery.
- **User Management:** Oversee customer accounts and delivery networks.

## Technology Stack
- **Frontend:** Next.js (React), Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion, Lucide React (Icons).
- **Backend:** Laravel (PHP) providing a robust API layer.
- **Database:** PostgreSQL for reliable, relational data storage.
