# 30-60-90 Day Growth & Scale Plan

## 1. 30-Day Launch Plan (Activation)
- **Goal**: Establish the marketplace flywheel (Liquidity).
- **Target**: 100 Active Sellers, 1,000 Customers, 5,000 Orders.
- **Focus**: High-touch seller onboarding. Resolving day-zero friction bugs. Aggressive customer acquisition via `FIRSTCATCH20` campaign.
- **Experimentation**: A/B testing the primary CTA on the homepage to optimize the signup funnel.

## 2. 60-Day Growth Plan (Optimization)
- **Goal**: Optimize Unit Economics and establish Retention.
- **Target**: 200 Active Sellers, 5,000 Customers, 25,000 Orders.
- **Focus**: Launching the Referral Program. Activating the AI Intelligence layer to track "At Risk" sellers and "At Risk" customers.
- **Experimentation**: Testing Dynamic Delivery Pricing (subsidizing delivery vs inflating product costs).

## 3. 90-Day Scale Plan (Expansion)
- **Goal**: Multi-Region Expansion and B2B Launch.
- **Target**: 500 Active Sellers, 15,000 Customers, 100,000 Orders.
- **Focus**: Expanding from Tamil Nadu into Kerala. Activating the B2B Procurement modules for restaurant chains. Launching the dedicated mobile applications for iOS/Android.
- **Experimentation**: Testing Subscription models (Weekly fresh catch deliveries).
