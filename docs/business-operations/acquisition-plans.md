# Marketplace Acquisition Strategies

## 1. Seller Acquisition & Success Program
- **Onboarding Workflow**: 
  - Application -> KYC Verification (GST/FSSAI) -> Admin Approval -> Virtual Training -> Profile Activation.
- **Success Tracking**: A seller is not "Successful" until they achieve their **First Completed Order**. Dedicated support agents will call sellers who remain inactive 72 hours after approval.
- **Top Seller Recognition**: Automated badging system (e.g., "Trusted Catch") for sellers maintaining < 1% defect rate and > 95% on-time fulfillment.

## 2. Customer Acquisition Foundation
- **Launch Campaign**: "Fresh from the net, straight to your kitchen." Geo-targeted Facebook/Instagram ads localized to Tamil Nadu coastal districts.
- **Coupon Strategy**: 
  - `FIRSTCATCH20`: 20% off the first order to reduce friction.
  - Cart Abandonment Coupons: Automatically emailed 24 hours after a drop-off.
- **Referral Program**: Double-sided incentives (Give ₹100, Get ₹100) credited to the internal platform wallet to ensure high virality and repeat purchasing.
- **Customer Retention Program**: 
  - *Loyalty Foundation*: Kadal Coins earned per purchase, redeemable on future orders.
  - *Reactivation*: Automated SMS to users who haven't ordered in 45 days.
